💡 How to Use This Guide

If this is your first time, follow the "Quick Start" steps below. Then check the "Complete Module Guide" section for a plain-language explanation of what every item in the left menu does — click a category name to expand it. To find a specific module quickly, use Ctrl+F (or your mobile browser's "Find on page") and type its name.

🚀 Quick Start
🏫 Step 1: Initial Setup

After logging in, go to Settings → General Settings and fill in your school information.

✅ Enter school name
✅ Add address, phone, email
✅ Upload school logo (from Admin tab → "Logo & Signature")
✅ Upload principal signature
✅ Click Save
👨‍🎓 Step 2: Adding Students
1. Click + New Student button
2. Enter name, class, roll, guardian name & phone
3. Click Save
4. Upload student photo if needed
💡 Use Excel Import to add multiple students at once
✅ Step 3: Taking Attendance
1. Go to Attendance from the left menu
2. Choose class and date
3. Mark each student ✅ Present / ❌ Absent
4. Click Save Attendance
5. Press Print to view the report
💰 Step 4: Fee Collection & Receipt
1. Go to Fee Management
2. Search student → click Collect Fee
3. Enter fee type and amount
4. Select payment method (Cash/bKash/Bank)
5. Print receipt — school logo & name auto-included
📝 Step 5: Exam Results & Report Cards
1. Go to Exam & Result
2. Enter exam name and date
3. Enter marks for each student
4. View report cards from the Marksheet page
5. Print — report card includes the school logo
